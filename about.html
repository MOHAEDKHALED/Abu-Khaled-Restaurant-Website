About Abu Khaled

Welcome to Abu Khaled, where we bring the heart of Gulf cuisine to your table. Founded in 2025, our family-run restaurant is dedicated to preserving the rich traditions of Gulf cooking, from fragrant kabsa to smoky mandi. Our recipes are crafted with love, using authentic spices and fresh ingredients to deliver an unforgettable dining experience. Whether you’re craving a hearty meal or a sweet treat like luqaimat, we’re here to make every bite a celebration of Gulf heritage.
